International Anomalies Society

Idea

The International Anomalies Society is a fun business idea based on selling products based on anomalies like aliens and ghosts. You could say this would be a funky online halloween store, but really, this business is about catering to an audience with an affinity for unordinary creatures. Some people (like me) may relate or connect to anomalies in ways that most people do not. With that being said, the IAS sells products of anomalies to connect to people who love anomalies and/or feel that they might just be one.

Plan

For a month or so, I will design the products for the IAS. After coming up with all the designs, I will buy the products using Printify so that I can use them for marketing photos and so that I can also check how the product quality could be improved on. Once everything for the designs are finalized, I will program an online store website for the IAS business. It will have a library of items to select from, a filter for searching, and a biographical page for the purpose of the website (containting contacts as well for feedback or help). When the basic elements of the online store is finished, I will focus on polishing the website up before starting to market the products. Even after marketing and basically putting the business out on the internet, I will continue working on improving the website and the products.

  1. Design a dozen of products: stickers, shirts, posters, and etc
  2. Create an online store for International Anomalies Society
  3. Market the products on Instagram, YouTube, and TikTok
